Coffee Trivia!

The discovery of coffee is often attributed to an Ethiopian legend involving a goat herder named Kaldi in the 9th century. According to the story, Kaldi observed
that his goats became more energetic after eating berries from a certain plant. Curious about the effects, Kaldi tried the berries himself and experienced a similar burst of energy. When was coffee first discovered?
